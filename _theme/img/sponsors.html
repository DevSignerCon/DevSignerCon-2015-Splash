DevSigner will bring together technology-oriented designers and invite them to share their expertise, helping them take Drupal front-end development to the next level. This is an opportunity for front-end developers to come together and learn from professionals beyond their spheres and explore new artistic directions for Drupal development, both classical and technical. This event will help creators expand their horizons through workshops and speaker presentations, as well as educate designers who would like to start creating for the web.
